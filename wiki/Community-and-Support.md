## GitHub

### Bug Reports and Feature Requests

If you find a bug or want to suggest a feature, open an issue on GitHub:

**[github.com/geograms/geogram/issues](https://github.com/geograms/geogram/issues)**

When reporting a bug, please include:
- What you were doing when it happened
- What you expected vs. what actually happened
- Your device and OS version
- Crash logs if available (from the app's log viewer or `logs/crash.txt` on a CLI station)
